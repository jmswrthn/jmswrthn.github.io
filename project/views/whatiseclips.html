What Is an Eclipse?

Video Source Location: rickonami on Youtube

A solar eclipse is a time period when the moon is between the earth and the sun , there are four types of eclipse
Total|Moons umbra and penumbra cast on Earth|
Partial|Only Moons penumbra cast on Earth|
Annular|Moons antumbra cast on earth only leaving outside ring of sun|
Hybrid|Total and Annular occur at same time
